Goal

You control a little planet lost in space. If you hit a lighter planet (in blue), you absorb its mass and grow. You loose when you're hit by a bigger planet (in red).

Controls

When you click/touch on screen, you go in the direction you've pointed. The farther from your planet you click/touch, the speeder you go to, but the more you consume you power bar. If you have no more power, you won't be able to move.
